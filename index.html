Characters Rate

Armin Arlert

Voice by Marina Inoue

He is a childhood friend of Eren Yeager and Mikasa Ackerman, and together they join the military to fight against the Titans.

Eren Yeager

Voice by Yuki Kaji

Eren's character undergoes significant development as he uncovers the truth about the Titans and his own identity.

Mikasa Ackerman

Voice by Yui Ishikawa

Mikasa is known for her exceptional fighting skills and devotion to Eren, whom she is fiercely protective of.

Levi Ackerman

Voice by Hiroshi Kamiya

He is the captain of the Survey Corps' Special Operations Squad and is considered one of the most skilled soldiers in the military.

Hange Zoë

Voice by Romi Park

She is a squad leader in the Survey Corps and is known for her intelligence, curiosity, and eccentric personality.

Erwin Smith

Voice by Daisuke Ono

He was the commander of the Survey Corps until his death, and is known for his exceptional strategic and leadership skills.

Jean Kirstein

Voice by Kisho Taniyama

Jean's character undergoes significant development throughout the series, particularly in relation to his beliefs and values.

Reiner Braun

Voice by Yoshimasa Hosoya

einer is known for his strong sense of duty and loyalty to his nation, even at the cost of his own personal beliefs and relationships.
